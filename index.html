Our Services Include

Comprehensive corporate capabilities visible at a glance.

Event Logistics

No more worrying about all the moving parts. Our team manages all the aspects of event logistics, from timeline management to onsite setup. We anticipate challenges before they happen.

Budgeting

Set your event goals and budget in confidence. We help clients build clear, flexible budgets that align with their goals, identifying cost-saving opportunities without compromising quality.

Strategy Design

Regardless of your event type, TCC will help you go from idea to reality. We work closely with you to understand your goals and design a strategic event from there.

Site Selection

Site selection is more than finding a fun property. It's about deeply understanding your goals, attendees, agenda and budget. TCC will help you discover and contract the ideal location.

Entertainment

From sophisticated receptions to high impact corporate events and bespoke social gatherings, we create seamless, memorable events that reflect your brand and exceed expectations.

Food & Beverage

Our F&B management services ensure exceptional culinary quality with operational efficiency. We oversee menu planning, cost control, vendor co-ordination and service execution.

Corporate Swag

Spoil your guests and reinforce your brand with gifting that goes beyond standard items. We help source customized branded attire, awards, trophies and plaques.

Speaker Mgmt

When speakers share their participation, they amplify your event's visibility. We connect you with professionals from diverse fields to generate excitement and credibility.

Vendor Management

We work with a wide range of vendors, securing dependable partners for decor, catering, and technical support to consistently deliver high-quality results.

Logistics (On & Off-Site)

Detailed coordination of transport, equipment, A/V, vendor flow and set up while maintaining strict planning behind the scenes. We deliver an organized operational flow.

Branding, Printing & Signage

We offer absolute creative production infrastructure including stage architectures, giant programmatic LED layouts, identity signages, custom motion graphics sequences, and branded attendee assets.
